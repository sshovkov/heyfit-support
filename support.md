  ## Frequently Asked Questions

  **How do I create a workout plan?**
  Go to Settings > Workout Plans > tap the + button to create a new plan. Add exercises with sets, reps, and weights.

  **How do I track a workout?**
  From the home screen, tap "Log" to quickly log a workout, or tap "Start" to follow along with a workout plan in real-time.

  **How do I delete my account?**
  Contact us at the email above and we'll process your request within 48 hours.

  **Is my data backed up?**
  Yes, your data is automatically synced to the cloud when you're connected to the internet.
