# Privacy Policy

  **Last updated: January 21, 2025**

  ## Overview

  HeyFit is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

  ## Information We Collect

  **Account Information**
  - Email address (for authentication)
  - Password (securely hashed, never stored in plain text)

  **Workout Data**
  - Workouts you log (date, activity type, notes)
  - Workout plans you create (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  - Weekly fitness goals

  ## How We Use Your Information

  - To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  - To sync your data across devices
  - To save your workout history and plans

  ## Data Storage

  Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

  ## Data Sharing

  We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your workout data is yours alone.

  ## Data Retention

  Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

  ## Your Rights

  You have the right to:
  - Access your personal data
  - Request correction of inaccurate data
  - Request deletion of your data
  - Export your data

  ## Children's Privacy

  HeyFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.

  ## Contact Us

  If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

  **Email:** heyfitworkout@gmail.com
